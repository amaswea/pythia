About Pythia

Pythia is a web service for interacting with and and viewing visualizations of course grade distribution data.

It is designed to help students make better decisions about the courses they take.
Pythia is different from other UW services in the following ways:
  • It enables viewing of historical grade distribution data since 2000
  • It enables filtering of that data by instructor, year, and quarter
  • It allows filtering in unique ways including filtering by students who have taken specific prerequisites, allowing the student to find similar student data
  • It finds a list of the top courses that students have taken at the same time as the course being viewed
  • Using course evaluation data, it aggregates the average number of hours per week students have spent on the course in the past
